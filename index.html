The gap

We opened equities. We are halfway through bonds. Nobody opened the money market.

A bank parks its spare rupee overnight and earns close to seven percent. A corporate treasury does the same. Their cash never sleeps. A small business keeps its working capital in a current account earning zero, while the same rupee, in the same hours, could be working. That is not a pricing problem. It is an access problem, and it sits underneath the whole of Indian finance.

What LiquiFi is

An autonomous treasury and liquidity layer, built for how lending and cash actually move in India.

LiquiFi forecasts cash, prices liquidity, scores credit, and routes idle balances through regulated rails, so a business earns on every rupee overnight and a lender sees a default before it lands. Calibrated to Indian money-market data, not retrofitted from a Western playbook.

The engine room

Six engines, each grounded in published research.

01

Forecasting

Multi horizon cash and liquidity forecasting, so the buffer is sized before the gap appears.

02

Liquidity at Risk

Monte Carlo simulation of shortfalls across thousands of paths over a rolling horizon.

03

Liquidity Credit Score

An LCR and NSFR driven score that reads buffer adequacy, funding stability and concentration.

04

Contagion Radar

Predicts which counterparties will delay payment before it lands, and sizes the line to pre arrange.

05

Liquidity Mesh

Matches surplus to deficit and prices it inside the bank spread, so both sides keep what the spread used to take.

06

Early Warning

Lender facing drift and breach detection, the monitoring a risk committee actually asks for.

Research grounded · India calibrated · built to survive a real risk review.

Why now

The rails are open. The rules are arriving. The access is the missing piece.

India built the payment rails and the regulator is rewriting how lenders manage risk. The plumbing to put idle cash to work and to watch a live book for trouble is the part still missing for everyone but the largest players. That is the gap LiquiFi was built to close.
